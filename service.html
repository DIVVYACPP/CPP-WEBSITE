Our Services

INGATE AUTOMATION

Automated system captures vehicle data, weighs the vehicle, and verifies driver details, making entry smooth and efficient.

PARKING

Initially had a 2,800-truck capacity, now adjusted for 2,200 with buffer and examination zones to enhance flow.

BUFFER

Provides secure storage for up to 4,500 containers during vessel delays, ensuring continuity in shipping operations.

REEFER POINTS

Facility with 135 plug points for refrigerated containers to keep perishable goods at optimal temperatures

CUSTOM HOUSE

Operates 24/7 for in-house customs with open examination for export containers to ensure compliance and secure trade.

CANTEEN

Has a capacity of 150 seats, offering meals and refreshments for drivers to support comfort during their stay.

DORMITORY

Provides a 50-bed facility for driver rest, enabling recovery from long hours on the road.

CLINIC

Supported by JNPA CSR, this clinic provides free essential medical services to drivers, focusing on their health and safety.

DRIVER TRAINING CENTER

Offers training on traffic rules and healthier lifestyles, emphasizing alcohol and addiction recovery for safer driving practices.

EXAMINATION

Containers marked for inspection go to an open examination area where contents are checked for compliance before export.
