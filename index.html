Centralized Parking Plaza (CPP)

Jawaharlal Nehru Port Authority (JNPA) has opened a new Centralized Parking Plaza (CPP) for container tractor trailers with Customs facilitation, as part of its efforts to promote ease of doing business. The parking plaza, spread across 45 hectares, has a capacity to park 2,200 tractor trailers at a time. The plaza has been built exclusively to integrate the parking of tractor trailers carrying factory stuffed export containers at one location instead of multiple locations earlier.

This will help integrate document processing by Customs with state-of-the-art facilities and service provision. JNPA is the only port in India which has planned for a facility of this scale, and CPP is a key initiative to streamline traffic movement and improve port efficiencies using IT services. At the same time, it provides convenience facilities and amenities at very nominal rates to truck drivers who travel long distances to reach the Port.

The Centralized Parking Plaza is an important part of our constant endeavor to provide ease of doing business within the Port. The plaza will be managed using a Real-Time Parking Management System and has WiFi provision within the premises. It features dormitories for truck drivers, canteens, well-maintained toilets, a vehicle repair area, and a commercial building. Guards and staff at entry gates ensure smooth entry formalities. JNPA has appointed Nirmala Auto Care Centre for operation, management, and maintenance of the Centralized Parking Plaza.

The plaza system will ease the movement of container trucks on JNPA roads and will help respective terminals plan their tractor trailer movements in a better way.

Under Ease of Business, the Centralized Parking Plaza offers the following services under one roof:

  • Vehicle Parking Capacity of 2,200 TTs.
  • In-House Customs House with 24/7 operation.
  • Dormitory facility for Drivers, with a capacity of 50 beds.
  • Free Health and Eye Check-Up Camps. Medical Insurance for Drivers.
  • Canteen facility with a seating capacity of 150.
  • Free Clinic for Drivers.
  • Hygienic Toilets and Washrooms.
  • Offices for Transporters/CHA's with Internet connectivity.
  • Customs Open Examination of Export Containers.
  • 130 nos. of Reefer Plug Points for Reefer Containers.
  • Buffer Zone for Containers in case of Vessel delay or Vessel cut-off. Capacity of Buffer Zone is 4,500 Containers.
  • CEAC Cell (Centralized Export Assessment Cell) for exporters by Customs.
  • FSP Cell (Factory Stuffing Permission Cell).
